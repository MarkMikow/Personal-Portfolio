Introduction

Welcome to Music Theory for Jazz Blues Guitar, a self-paced 40-Lesson course for developing your understanding of music and your guitar prowess! Music Theory for Jazz Blues Guitar is being presented lesson-by-lesson, and this website is continuously being updated with new lessons.

There will be three versions of Music Theory for Jazz Blues Guitar:
1) the website version,
2) an ebook version, which is soon-to-be available on Amazon Kindle, and
3) a pdf version, which you will be able to download directly from the website.

The website version will contain all the lessons, along with a few exercises for each lesson. Each lesson will be given in the key of C and will contain both music manuscript and tablature.

The Kindle ebook will contain all the lessons as well, plus a multitude of exercises. Each exercise will be given in all 12 keys in both manuscript and tab form.

The pdf version will be similar to the ebook, though you will be able to easily print it at home.
